The Festival of Faith & Devotion

EThe Sacred Rituals

Chhath Puja is a four-day festival dedicated to the Sun God and Chhathi Maiya. Devotees perform rigorous rituals and fasting to thank the divine for sustaining life on earth..

Evening Arghya

The evening offerings to the setting sun symbolize gratitude and devotion. Devotees stand in water bodies offering prayers and prasad to the Sun God.

Morning Arghya

The final morning offering marks the conclusion of Chhath Puja, where devotees offer prayers to the rising sun, symbolizing new beginnings and blessings.

Amazing Facts About Chhath Puja

🌅

Ancient Tradition

Dating back to the Vedic times, Chhath Puja is one of the oldest Hindu festivals dedicated to the Sun God (Surya) and Chhathi Maiya.

🙏

36-Hour Fast

Devotees observe an incredible nirjala vrat (without water) for 36 hours! This showcases their unwavering devotion and spiritual strength.

🌊

Eco-Friendly

One of the most eco-friendly festivals where offerings are biodegradable and celebrations promote environmental harmony.
